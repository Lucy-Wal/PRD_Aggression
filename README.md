### Data

#### Data Structure:
- ID
- PRDS1-PRDS5: Personal Relative Deprivation Scale item responses (range 1-6, already reverse scored where appropriate)
- DASS1-DASS21: Depression, Anxiety, and Stress Scale item responses (range 1-4)
- SCS1_PC-SCS12_PC: Sense of Control Scale item responses indicating Perceived Constraints (_PC) or Personal Mastery (_PM) sub scale (range 1-7, already reverse scored)
- BPAQ1-BPAQ12: Buss-Perry Aggression Questionnaire item responses (range 1-5, already reverse scored where appropriate)
- Income: Annual Household Income before taxes (1 = 10,000 GPB or less a year, 23 = More than 150,000 GBP a year)
- Gender: 1 = Male, 2 = Female, 3 = Non-Binary, 4 = Prefer Not To Say
- Age: Years
- PC: Mean score of Perceived Constraint items in the Sense of Control Scale
- PM: Mean score of Personal Mastery items in the Sense of Control Scale
- DASS: Mean score of all items in the Depression, Anxiety, and Stress Scale
- PRDS: Mean score of all items in the Personal Relative Deprivation Scale
- BPAQ: Mean score of all items in the Buss-Perry Aggression Questionnaire
